Why "Ravana"?

In Indian mythology, Ravana's 10 heads are often interpreted as a symbol of his vast knowledge and wisdom across many fields. This app, in the same spirit, brings together the "wisdom" of multiple AIs under one roof, allowing you to leverage their collective power seamlessly.
